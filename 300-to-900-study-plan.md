# TOEIC Trail 300 to 900 Study Plan

## Starting Point
- Current score: around 300+
- Current vocabulary: about 2,000-3,000 words
- Main gaps: TOEIC collocations, listening reaction speed, Part 5 grammar decisions, Part 7 scanning speed

## Daily Minimum
- 40 vocabulary cards
- 30 practice questions
- 10 listening replay/shadowing prompts
- 1 mistake review session

## Phase 1: 300 to 500
Duration: 4-6 weeks

Focus:
- Foundation vocabulary
- Basic verbs and workplace nouns
- Part 5 basic grammar
- Part 2 who / where / when / why questions

Use in app:
- 300 基礎補強
- 聽力精聽
- 基礎字庫

## Phase 2: 500 to 700
Duration: 6-8 weeks

Focus:
- Word forms
- Prepositions
- Business collocations
- Single-passage Part 7 reading

Use in app:
- 每日 30 題
- 閱讀掃描
- 錯題本

## Phase 3: 700 to 850
Duration: 6-8 weeks

Focus:
- Part 6 text completion
- Part 7 inference
- Listening distractors
- Time pressure

Use in app:
- 模擬測驗
- 聽力精聽
- 閱讀掃描

## Phase 4: 850 to 900+
Duration: 4-8 weeks

Focus:
- Full mock rhythm
- Weak area repair
- High-frequency synonyms
- Review all mistakes

Use in app:
- 模擬測驗
- 錯題本
- 成績與弱點分析
